Financial Modeling & Forecasting Foundational
Q-001

Walk me through a DCF valuation.

A DCF values a company based on the present value of its projected free cash flows. I'd project UFCF for 5-10 years, calculate a terminal value, and discount both back using WACC to find Enterprise Value.
Strategic Framework: Assumptions dictate everything. Revenue growth, margin assumptions, CapEx, and working capital changes drive the UFCF. WACC blends Cost of Equity (CAPM) and Cost of Debt.
