# 📊 Corporate Finance Leadership Interview Playbook
**University of Utah · David Eccles School of Business**

## 📌 Platform Overview
Welcome to the **Eccles MBA Corporate Finance Leadership Interview Playbook**. This platform is a self-contained, interactive training simulator designed specifically for MBA candidates pursuing competitive roles in Corporate Finance, FP&A, and Strategic Finance. 

The playbook features **125 curated interview questions**, core valuation frameworks, and a live **AI Speech Coaching engine** that grades candidate verbal responses in real-time based on cadence, keyword density, and filler-word tracking.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started (No Installation Required)
Because this tool is built as a self-contained HTML application, there are no software installations, dependencies, or logins required.

1. **Download the File:** Clone this repository or download the `Eccles_Corporate_Finance_Playbook.html` file directly to your local machine.
2. **Launch the Playbook:** Double-click the file. It will securely open and run locally within your default web browser.
3. **Browser Requirements:** For the AI Speech Coaching engine to function correctly, **you must use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge**. (Safari and Firefox do not currently support the native webkit speech recognition API required for the AI engine).

---

## ⚙️ Core Features & Navigation

### 1. 📚 The Question Bank
The core of the platform is the interactive question bank. Questions are tagged by category (e.g., *Financial Modeling*, *FP&A*) and difficulty (*Foundational*, *Core*, *Advanced*).
* **Conversational Answer Tab:** Provides a natural, spoken-word example of how to successfully answer the prompt in an executive interview setting.
* **Deep Dive Tab:** Reveals the underlying strategic framework, mathematical formulas, or recruiter intent behind the question.

### 2. 🎤 AI Practice Studio
This tool acts as your live mock-interview coach. 
* Click **"Practice & Grade Answer"** under any question to open the studio.
* Click **"Start Recording"** and speak your answer out loud. The engine will capture your speech in real-time. *(Note: The engine is designed to wait if you pause to think; it will not erase your text).*
* When finished, click **"Submit for AI Evaluation"**.

**The Scoring Heuristic:**
The internal JavaScript engine analyzes the transcript and dynamically generates a score (0–100) based on:
1. **Length & Depth:** Ensuring the answer is robust enough for an executive interview (targeting 80+ words).
2. **Keyword Density:** Rewarding the use of core MBA finance terminology (e.g., *WACC, EBITDA, synergies, variance, terminal value*).
3. **Filler Word Penalty:** Deducting points for excessive use of filler words (e.g., *um, uh, like*).

### 3. 📊 Readiness Dashboard
The dashboard updates automatically every time a candidate submits an answer to the AI Practice Studio.
* **Overall Score:** Cumulative average across all practice sessions.
* **Readiness Level:** A dynamic indicator showing current standing (*Emerging*, *Developing*, *Interview Ready*, *Offer Ready*).
* **Competency Bars:** Visual trackers indicating strength in specific financial domains.
* **Average Filler Words:** Tracks speaking habits to help candidates sound more polished and executive.

> *To start over, simply click the **"🔄 Reset Progress"** button in the top right corner of the header.*

## 💡 Best Practices for Preparation
To extract the maximum value from this playbook, we recommend the following workflow:

1. **The Solo Audit (Days 1-3):** Read through the Conversational Answers for the *Foundational* questions to baseline your current knowledge.
2. **The Framework Drill (Days 4-7):** Study the Finance Frameworks tab. Do not memorize answers; instead, memorize the *structure* of the answers (e.g., the 3-Statement Linking Blueprint).
3. **The Simulator (Days 8+):** Use the AI Practice Studio daily. Aim to get the "Average Filler Words" below 2 per session and the "Overall Readiness Score" consistently above an 85.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

* **My microphone isn't picking up my voice:** Ensure you are using Chrome or Edge. Look at the right side of your URL address bar; if there is a camera/microphone icon with a red 'X', click it and select *"Allow microphone access for this site."*
* **My scores are saving, but I want to start over:** Click the "Reset Progress" button in the top header. **Warning:** This will wipe your local cache and cannot be undone. 
* **The page looks zoomed in or elements are cutting off:** Press `Ctrl + 0` (Windows) or `Cmd + 0` (Mac) to reset your browser zoom to exactly 100%.
